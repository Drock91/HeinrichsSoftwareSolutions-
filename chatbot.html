We carefully train the chatbot on accurate business info and test it before launch. We also include monthly prompt tuning to improve accuracy over time. The bot is designed to say "I don't know, please contact us" rather than guess.

Yes! Email us any changes (new services, updated hours, new pricing) and we'll update your bot, usually within 24 hours. Monthly updates are included in all plans.
