About Us

Veteran-founded. Mission-driven. Built for excellence.

Veteran-Founded. Mission-Driven.

Heinrichs Software Solutions Company was founded by Derek Heinrichs, a U.S. Navy veteran with 8+ years of active duty as a Navy Diver and 10,000+ hours of programming experience. We bring the same level of excellence, discipline, and accountability to AI solutions that the military demands of its service members.

Based in Florida, we specialize in AI-powered tools for businesses — starting with our flagship AI chatbot product. We build, deploy, and manage AI solutions on serverless cloud infrastructure so our clients can focus on running their business while their AI works 24/7.

Our core philosophy is simple: every solution we build must deliver real value to the clients who depend on it. We primarily serve small and mid-sized businesses, and we're also SDVOSB certified for government contracts.
