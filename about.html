About Us

Veteran-founded. Mission-driven. Built for government-grade excellence.

Veteran-Founded. Mission-Driven.

Heinrichs Software Solutions LLC was founded by Derek Heinrichs, a 100% service-connected disabled veteran, with a single mission: deliver the same level of excellence, discipline, and accountability to the software industry that the U.S. military demands of its service members.

Based in Florida, we are a registered Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) on SAM.gov, qualified for SDVOSB and Small Business set-aside contracts. We specialize in building the mission-critical software that federal agencies need — on time, on budget, and to specification.

Our core philosophy is simple: every line of code we ship must be worthy of the agencies and warfighters who depend on it.

Core Capabilities

  • Full-Stack Software Development
  • Cloud Architecture & Migration (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Cybersecurity & Security Hardening
  • API Development & Systems Integration
  • DevOps, CI/CD & Infrastructure Automation
  • Web & Mobile Application Development
  • Data Analytics & Visualization
