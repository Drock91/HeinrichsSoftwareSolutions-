Cookie Policy

Understanding how we use cookies on our website

Effective Date: March 20, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 20, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Heinrichs Software Solutions Company, LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website at heinrichstech.com (the "Site"). This policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy, which explains how we use personal information.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide information to website owners, and enable certain features.

Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies:

  • Persistent cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them
  • Session cookies are deleted when you close your web browser

Cookies can also be "first-party" (set by us) or "third-party" (set by other companies whose services we use).

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies (Strictly Necessary)

These cookies are required for the Site to function properly and cannot be disabled. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility.

Analytics Cookies (Performance)

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our Site by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our Site.

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to analyze traffic and usage patterns. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit the Site, what pages they visit, and what other sites they used prior to coming to the Site. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use the following similar technologies:

  • Local Storage: We use browser local storage to store data locally on your device, such as authentication tokens and user preferences. Unlike cookies, local storage data is not sent to our servers with every request.
  • Session Storage: Similar to local storage but cleared when you close your browser. Used for temporary data during your browsing session.
  • Pixel Tags / Web Beacons: Small graphic images that may be included in our marketing emails to track whether emails are opened and links are clicked.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our Site are placed by third parties. We use services from the following third parties that may set cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Web analytics service. Privacy Policy
  • Stripe: Payment processing (on checkout pages). Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts: Web fonts delivery. Privacy Policy

We do not control how these third parties use cookies. Please review their privacy policies for more information.
