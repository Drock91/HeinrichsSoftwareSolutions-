About Us

Veteran-Founded. Mission-Driven.

Heinrichs Software Solutions LLC was founded by Derek Heinrichs, a 100% service-connected disabled veteran, with a single mission: deliver the same level of excellence, discipline, and accountability to the software industry that the U.S. military demands of its service members.

Based in Florida, we are a registered SDVOSB on SAM.gov, qualified for SDVOSB and Small Business set-aside contracts.
